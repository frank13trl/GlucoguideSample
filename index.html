What is Glucoguide?

Glucoguide is the device we developed to ensure the health of diabetic patients in a more healthier and safer way. It uses non-evasive methods to measue the glucose level in blood. It means that you don't have to prick anymore!

This website uses the Glucoguide device to assess the patients' health and provide them with help and support to live healthier lives. This website bring together the patients and doctors where they can communicate with each other.

We believe you deserve superior healthcare and an excellent patient experience, and we are committed to living our values. We believe you deserve to be treated with compassion and respect. We believe that communication with our patients and members should be timely, open and honest. And we believe you deserve affordable, coordinated, high-quality care and services that are easy to find and simple to use. It's what we do best.
